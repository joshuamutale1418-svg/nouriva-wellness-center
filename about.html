About Nouriva Wellness Centre

Nouriva Wellness Centre is a Lusaka-based holistic health practice focused on empowering individuals through nutrition, lifestyle, and compassionate wellness support.

Founder's Story

Founded by Ms. Annastasia M.Luundu, After overcoming long COVID, Chornic, fatigue, and other health related challenges, she turned her personal healing journey into a mission: empowering others to live healthier balanced lives. At Nouriva, we believe in restoring health energy, vitality and balance through nutrition, lifestyle and compassionate care.
